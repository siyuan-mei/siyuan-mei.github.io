Research Interests

Image Reconstruction

Improving the quality and accuracy of medical scans through advanced algorithmic pipelines.

Generative Models

Harnessing Diffusion Models and GANs for image synthesis, inpainting, and denoising in clinical data.

Image Registration

Developing efficient methods for multi-modal and high-resolution image alignment.

Featured Project: ERC 4D-Nanoscope

Advancing osteoporosis medicine by observing bone microstructure and remodelling using a four-dimensional nanoscope. This project aims to push the boundaries of how we visualize bone health at the micro-scale.
