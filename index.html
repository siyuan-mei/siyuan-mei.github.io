About Me

Siyuan Mei 梅思远

Hello! I'm Siyuan Mei, a third-year PhD student at Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg (FAU), where I'm fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Andreas Maier. Previously, I received my M.Sc. degree from FAU and my B.Eng. degree from Zhejiang University of Science and Technology. I'm part of the ERC-funded project "Advancing osteoporosis medicine by observing bone microstructure.". My research focuses on machine learning and medical imaging.

You can contact me at siyuan.mei@fau.de and view my resume here.

Outside of research, I am learning tennis (level of 2.5).
